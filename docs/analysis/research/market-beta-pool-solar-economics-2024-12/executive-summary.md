# Executive Summary

### Key Findings

**Pool Ownership (MKT-03):**
- **Total Annual Cost:** $2,500 - $4,800/year for professional maintenance; $1,200 - $2,400/year DIY
- **Monthly Breakdown:** $250 - $400/month comprehensive ownership cost
- **Critical Factor:** Equipment replacement cycles (pump 8-12 yrs, heater 11-15 yrs) add significant long-term costs
- **Arizona Advantage:** No winterization costs, but higher evaporation and pump runtime

**Solar Economics (MKT-04):**
- **Leased Solar:** Decreases home value by 3-8% in Phoenix [High Confidence]
- **Owned Solar:** Increases home value by 4.1-6.9% nationally [High Confidence]
- **Lease Prevalence:** ~75% of Phoenix-area solar installations are leased
- **Escalator Risk:** 2-3% annual payment increases over 20-25 year terms
- **Buyout Costs:** $9,000 - $21,000+ depending on contract terms

### PHX Houses Scoring Recommendations

| Factor | Current Treatment | Recommended Update |
|--------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Pool | Cost factor in monthly budget | Add $300-400/month to cost projections |
| Solar Lease | Kill-switch consideration | HARD FAIL recommended (3-8% value reduction) |
| Owned Solar | Not scored | Consider +5 pts to Location/Systems score |
