# screenshots

## Purpose
Stores browser screenshots captured during image extraction for debugging and verification of stealth browser automation workflows.

## Contents
| Path | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `*.png` | Browser screenshots from extraction runs |
| `*.jpg` | Alternative format screenshots |

## Usage
- Screenshots auto-generated by `StealthBrowserExtractor` during extraction
- Used for debugging CAPTCHA detection, page navigation issues
- Review when extraction fails to identify visual blockers

## Retention
- Screenshots may be cleaned up periodically
- Keep recent screenshots for debugging active issues
- Archive or delete screenshots older than 7 days
